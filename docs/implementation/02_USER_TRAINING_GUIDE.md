## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Accessing the Platform
1. Open https://ceo-platform.example.com
2. Click "Login" or "Register"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Verify your email (check inbox)
5. You're ready to start!

### First Time Setup
1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Click on your avatar (top right)
   - Select "Profile Settings"
   - Add your phone, company info
   - Save changes

2. **Add Payment Method**
   - Go to "Settings" → "Payment Methods"
   - Add your preferred payment method
   - Mark as default

3. **Explore Products**
   - Click "Browse Products"
   - Search for what you need
   - Add items to cart
   - Proceed to checkout

## Part 1: Browsing & Ordering

### Searching for Products

**Text Search**:
1. Go to "Products" → "Search"
2. Type product name or keyword
3. Press Enter or click Search
4. Results show immediately

**Browse by Category**:
1. Go to "Products"
2. Click category on left sidebar
3. Sub-categories appear if available
4. Click to filter

**Advanced Filters**:
1. Go to "Products"
2. Click "Filters" button
3. Filter by:
   - Price range
   - Category
   - Supplier/Firm
   - Availability
4. Click "Apply Filters"

### Understanding Product Information

**Product Card Shows**:
- **Product Name**: Click to view details
- **Image**: Product preview
- **Price**: Current price (may vary by quantity)
- **Stock**: Availability indicator
- **Unit**: Sold in units (kg, box, etc.)
- **Minimum Quantity**: Minimum order quantity

**Product Details Page**:
- Full description
- Specification details
- All available price tiers
- Customer reviews
- Supplier information

### Price Tiers Explained

Products may have different prices based on quantity ordered:

**Example**:
- 1-10 units: $10/unit
- 11-50 units: $8/unit
- 51-100 units: $6/unit

The more you order, the lower the price!

### Adding to Cart

1. Go to product page
2. Select quantity (must meet minimum)
3. Click "Add to Cart"
4. Confirm in popup
5. View cart to proceed

## Part 2: Shopping Cart & Checkout

### Managing Your Cart

1. Click cart icon (top right)
2. View all items
3. **Modify items**:
   - Change quantity: Click +/- buttons
   - Remove: Click trash icon
   - Save to Order: Click "Save to Order"
4. **View total**: Shows subtotal, taxes, total
5. **Proceed**: Click "Checkout"

### Checkout Process

**Step 1: Review Items**
- Verify quantities and prices
- Adjust if needed
- Click "Continue"

**Step 2: Delivery Address**
- Confirm or change delivery address
- Must be valid for your region
- Click "Continue"

**Step 3: Billing Information**
- Confirm or update
- Used for invoicing
- Click "Continue"

**Step 4: Payment Method**
- Select payment method
- **Cash**: Pay on delivery
- **Monthly Billing**: Added to invoice
- Click "Continue"

**Step 5: Review & Confirm**
- Review all information
- Click "Place Order"
- Receive order confirmation

### Order Confirmation

After placing order, you'll see:
- Order number (reference for support)
- Order status (Pending → Confirmed → Shipped)
- Estimated delivery date
- Total amount

You'll also receive:
- Email confirmation
- Tracking information (when shipped)
- Invoice (at month-end)

## Part 3: Monthly Invoicing

### How Monthly Invoicing Works

**Billing Cycle**:
- Billing period: 1st to last day of each month
- Invoice generated: 1st-3rd day of following month
- Invoice sent: Via email + accessible in dashboard
- Payment due: Within 30 days (terms may vary)

### Viewing Your Invoices

1. Go to "My Invoices"
2. View all invoices for your account
3. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Status (Draft, Sent, Confirmed, Paid)
   - Amount

**Invoice Contains**:
- All orders from billing period
- Item quantities and prices
- Subtotal and taxes
- Total amount due

### Invoice Statuses

| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Draft | Being prepared | Check back soon |
| Sent | Sent to you | Review and confirm |
| Confirmed | You reviewed it | Payment due in 30 days |
| Paid | Payment received | Archived |

### Confirming an Invoice

When invoice sent to you:
1. Receive email notification
2. Log in and open invoice
3. Review all items and amounts
4. Click "Confirm Invoice"
5. Invoice marked as confirmed
6. Payment due in 30 days

### Downloading Invoice

1. Go to "My Invoices"
2. Find invoice in list
3. Click "Download PDF"
4. Save to your computer
5. Share with finance team if needed

## Part 4: Group Buying (Special Feature)

### What is Group Buying?

Group buying allows teams to combine orders for better pricing:
- **Lower unit costs** through volume discounts
- **Group leader** manages the group
- **All team members** participate and benefit
- **Rebate** earned by group from supplier

### Starting a Group

1. Go to "Groups" → "Create New Group"
2. Fill in details:
   - Group name
   - Description (optional)
   - Deadline for joining
3. Click "Create Group"
4. You're the group leader

### Joining a Group

1. Go to "Groups"
2. View available groups
3. Click "Join Group"
4. Review group details
5. Confirm joining

### Adding Items to Group

As group member:
1. Go to "Groups" → "My Groups"
2. Select group
3. Click "Add Items"
4. Browse and add products
5. Specify your quantity
6. Click "Add to Group"

As group leader:
- Can see all members' orders
- Monitor total items
- See projected group rebate
- Can close group early if desired

### Group Deadline & Finalizing

- **Before deadline**: Members can add/modify items
- **Deadline reached**: Group closes, no more additions
- **After deadline**: Group rebate calculated
- **Rebate invoice**: Issued to group leader (typically 2-5% of total)

### Rebate Distribution

Group leader typically:
1. Receives rebate invoice from company
2. Distributes rebate amount to members
3. Can use platform dashboard to track who bought what
4. (Manual distribution - not automated)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click link and set new password
5. Log in with new password

**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A:
1. Go to Settings → Profile
2. Click "Change Email"
3. Enter new email
4. Verify new email (check inbox)
5. Old email is no longer active

**Q: I'm not receiving login emails. What do I do?**
A:
- Check spam folder
- Whitelist ceo-platform@example.com
- Contact support if still having issues
- We can resend verification email

### Products & Ordering

**Q: How do I know if a product is in stock?**
A:
- Check product page - stock status shown
- Green = In stock
- Yellow = Limited stock
- Red = Out of stock
- Contact supplier via product page if out of stock

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A:
- If status is "Pending": Yes, cancel anytime
- If status is "Confirmed": Contact support
- If status is "Shipped": Too late, contact support for return

**Q: What if I ordered wrong quantity?**
A:
- Pending status: Edit quantity in cart
- Confirmed/Shipped: Contact support for exchange

### Invoicing & Payment

**Q: When do I get my invoice?**
A:
- Generated 1-3 days after month ends
- Sent via email automatically
- Also accessible in "My Invoices" dashboard

**Q: How long do I have to pay my invoice?**
A:
- Standard terms: 30 days from invoice date
- Check invoice for specific due date
- Late payments may incur fees

**Q: Can I pay early?**
A:
- Cash payments: Only at delivery
- Monthly billing: Contact accounts payable
- Early payment not available through platform

**Q: I see a mistake in my invoice. What do I do?**
A:
1. Contact support with invoice number
2. Describe the discrepancy
3. Support team will investigate
4. May issue credit/corrected invoice

### Group Buying

**Q: What's the difference between regular and group orders?**
A:
- **Regular**: Individual purchase, standard pricing
- **Group**: Combined with others, volume discounts, potential rebate

**Q: How much is the group rebate?**
A:
- Varies by supplier and volume
- Typically 2-5% of group total
- Calculated after group deadline
- Issued to group leader

**Q: Can I join multiple groups?**
A:
- Yes, join multiple groups for same product
- But each group has separate deadline
- Manage items in each group separately

**Q: What if the group leader removes me?**
A:
- You're notified via email
- Your items stay in group until deadline
- You receive portion of rebate still
- Contact support if concerned
