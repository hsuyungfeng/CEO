## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login (Most Common)

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A:
1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click link and create new password
6. Log back in with new password

Time to resolve: 5 minutes

---

**Q: I'm not receiving verification emails after registration.**
A:
- Check spam/junk folder first
- Add support@ceo-platform.example.com to contacts
- Try different email if available
- **Support action**:
  - Resend verification email manually
  - Check email delivery logs
  - Create account manually if needed

Time to resolve: 10 minutes

---

**Q: Someone else accessed my account. What should I do?**
A: (Security Issue - P1)
1. **Immediate actions**:
   - Change password immediately
   - Review recent orders
   - Check for unauthorized transactions
2. **Contact support immediately**:
   - Provide affected time period
   - List suspicious activities
3. **Support will**:
   - Audit account access logs
   - Revert fraudulent orders
   - Force re-authentication
   - Review security

Time to resolve: 30 minutes

---

**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A:
1. Log in to account
2. Go to Settings → Profile
3. Click "Change Email"
4. Verify current email
5. Enter new email
6. Verify new email (check inbox)
7. Old email is no longer usable

Time to resolve: 15 minutes

### Ordering & Products (High Volume)

**Q: I can't add an item to my cart. What's wrong?**
A:
**Most common causes**:
1. **Minimum quantity not met**: Some products require minimum order qty
   - Solution: Increase quantity to meet minimum
2. **Out of stock**: Product currently unavailable
   - Solution: Contact supplier, check back later
3. **Browser issue**: Cache or cookies blocking action
   - Solution: Refresh page, clear cache, try different browser

If none work:
- **Support action**: Check server logs, manually add item, account investigation

Time to resolve: 10 minutes

---

**Q: The price shown is different from what I paid. Why?**
A:
**Common reasons**:
1. **Price tiers**: Actual price depends on quantity ordered
   - Solution: Check if you ordered less than lower-tier quantity
2. **Taxes added**: Price shown is before tax
   - Solution: Check invoice for tax breakdown
3. **Currency conversion**: Different regions have different pricing
   - Solution: Verify your region setting

If price is wrong:
- **Support action**: Review order, issue credit if overcharged

Time to resolve: 20 minutes

---

**Q: Can I change my order after placing it?**
A:
**Depends on order status**:
- **Pending** (not yet confirmed): Yes, edit through cart
- **Confirmed** (approved by us): Contact support for changes
- **Shipped**: Too late, contact support about return/exchange

**Support can**:
- Modify quantities (before shipped)
- Add items to order (before shipped)
- Approve address changes (before shipped)
- Process returns/exchanges (if shipped)

Time to resolve: 15 minutes

---

**Q: I was charged but didn't receive my order. Where is it?**
A:
1. **Check order status** in platform:
   - Pending: Being processed
   - Confirmed: Approved for shipping
   - Shipped: In transit (check tracking)
   - Completed: Received
2. **If status is wrong**:
   - **Support will**: Investigate, check warehouse, provide update
3. **If lost in shipping**:
   - **Support will**: Contact carrier, file claim, reship

Time to resolve: 1-3 days (depending on shipping carrier)

### Invoicing & Billing

**Q: When will I receive my monthly invoice?**
A:
- Invoices generated 1-3 days after month ends
- Automatically sent via email
- Also available in platform under "My Invoices"
- Standard billing cycle: 1st-last day of month
- Invoice issued: Next 1-3 days

Time to resolve: Informational

---

**Q: I don't see some items on my invoice.**
A:
**Possible reasons**:
1. **Items still pending**: Orders status must be "Confirmed" or "Shipped"
   - Solution: Wait for order confirmation
2. **Different billing period**: Item from different month
   - Solution: Check invoice for correct month
3. **Item already invoiced**: From previous month
   - Solution: Check previous month's invoice
4. **Actual error**: Item should be on invoice but missing
   - **Support will**: Investigate database, issue corrected invoice

Time to resolve: 15-30 minutes

---

**Q: Can I get a copy of an old invoice?**
A:
**Self-service**:
1. Log in to account
2. Go to "My Invoices"
3. Find invoice by month/date
4. Click "Download PDF"
5. Save to computer

**If lost/missing**:
- Contact support with month needed
- Support will email copy
- Archives available for up to 3 years

Time to resolve: 5 minutes (self-service) / 10 minutes (support)

---

**Q: My invoice has an error. How do I dispute it?**
A:
1. **Document the error**: Note specific line items, amounts
2. **Contact support**: Email with screenshot of error
3. **Provide proof**: Order confirmation, receipt, etc.
4. **Support will**:
   - Investigate discrepancy
   - Verify against orders
   - Issue credit or corrected invoice
   - Explain what happened

Time to resolve: 24-48 hours

### Group Buying

**Q: How do I join a group buying order?**
A:
1. Go to "Groups" section
2. View "Available Groups"
3. Find group you want to join
4. Click "Join Group"
5. Review group details (deadline, members, items)
6. Confirm joining
7. You can now add items to group

Time to resolve: 5 minutes

---

**Q: What's the group rebate and how do I get my share?**
A:
**How it works**:
- Group combines orders for volume discount
- Supplier gives rebate (typically 2-5% of total)
- **Group leader receives** rebate invoice
- **Group leader distributes** rebate to members
- Amount depends on final group size and total

**Common questions**:
- **Q: How much will the rebate be?**
  - A: Varies by supplier and group size. Estimated amount shown in group.
- **Q: When do I get my rebate?**
  - A: After group deadline closes and final orders confirmed (~1-2 weeks)
- **Q: Can I refuse the rebate?**
  - A: Not applicable - only group leader receives rebate

**Support can**:
- Explain rebate calculation
- Verify rebate received
- Help distribute if group leader requests

Time to resolve: 15 minutes

---

**Q: I want to leave a group. Can I?**
A:
**Before group deadline**:
- Go to "Groups" → "My Groups"
- Find group
- Click "Leave Group"
- Your items removed from group
- You can join another group instead

**After group deadline**:
- Group has closed to new additions
- If items already processed, contact support to remove
- Rebate still applies to your portion

**Support can**:
- Remove you from group
- Process item refunds if needed
- Add you to different group

Time to resolve: 10 minutes

### Technical Issues

**Q: The website is loading very slowly. What's wrong?**
A:
**Most common causes**:
1. **Browser cache**: Outdated files loaded
   - Solution: Clear cache (Ctrl+Shift+Del)
   - Solution: Refresh page (Ctrl+R)
2. **Internet connection**: Slow connection
   - Solution: Check connection speed
   - Solution: Try different network
3. **Server under load**: High traffic
   - Solution: Try again in a few minutes
4. **Browser extension**: Blocking content
   - Solution: Disable extensions, try incognito mode

**If still slow**:
- **Support can**:
  - Check server performance
  - Verify your ISP connection
  - Investigate specific page
  - Escalate if server issue

Time to resolve: 10 minutes

---

**Q: I'm seeing an error message. What does it mean?**
A:
**Common error codes**:

| Error | Meaning | Solution |
|-------|---------|----------|
| 400 | Bad Request | Invalid data sent. Check form fields. |
| 401 | Unauthorized | Not logged in. Log in again. |
| 403 | Forbidden | Don't have permission. Contact support. |
| 404 | Not Found | Page doesn't exist. Check URL. |
| 500 | Server Error | System error. Try again. Contact support if persists. |
| 503 | Service Down | System maintenance. Check status page. |

**Support can**:
- Explain error message
- Provide workaround
- Escalate to engineering if technical

Time to resolve: 10 minutes

---

**Q: The payment form won't accept my credit card. Why?**
A:
**Common causes**:
1. **Card expired**: Check expiration date
2. **Card blocked**: Contact your bank about fraud alerts
3. **Insufficient funds**: Verify account balance
4. **Incorrect info**: Re-enter card number, CVV, zip code
5. **Card not supported**: Not all cards accepted internationally

**Solutions**:
- Try different payment method
- Try different browser
- Clear cookies and retry
- Contact your bank to unblock

**Support can**:
- Process payment manually (email payment details securely)
- Investigate payment processor issues
- Arrange alternative payment

Time to resolve: 15-30 minutes

## Escalation Procedure

### When to Escalate

**Escalate to Engineering when**:
- Technical error not in KB
- Database integrity concern
- API endpoint not responding
- Security-related issue
- Multiple customers affected

**Escalation form**:
```
Severity: [P0/P1/P2/P3]
Issue: [Brief description]
Steps to reproduce: [Detailed steps]
Affected users: [Number and list]
Screenshots/logs: [Attachments]
Customer impact: [What can't they do?]
```

### Escalation Chain
1. **Support Team** (first line, 30 min response)
2. **Engineering Lead** (if not resolved in 1 hour)
3. **CTO** (if P0, immediate)

## Troubleshooting Scripts for Support

```bash
# Check in database if user exists and active
psql -c "SELECT id, email, status FROM users WHERE email='user@example.com';" $DATABASE_URL
```

```bash
# Check invoices for user
psql -c "SELECT id, invoiceNo, billingMonth, status FROM invoices WHERE userId='<userId>';" $DATABASE_URL
```
